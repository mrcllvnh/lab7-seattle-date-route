Seattle Date Route

Afternoon → Evening (Fremont to Capitol Hill)

This GeoNarrative maps a simple Seattle date route in two scenes: a calm afternoon around Lake Union and Fremont, followed by a lively evening in Capitol Hill. Scroll to transition between scenes and see the map update.

GEOG Lab 7 • Marcella Van Herry

Afternoon: Fremont + Lake Union

We start the date slow and scenic—skyline views, a lakeside walk, and time to wander Fremont’s quirky streets. This part of the route is about open space, daylight, and exploring at an easy pace.

Evening: Capitol Hill

By evening, the mood shifts to brighter streets and more energy. Capitol Hill is perfect for dinner, dessert, and a night walk through neon-lit blocks. This scene ends the route with the social side of Seattle.
